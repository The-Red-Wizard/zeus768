v3.4.5 (January 2026)
================================================================================
  * NEW: Kids Zone now has same Netflix-style layout as Movies and TV Shows
    - Netflix-style submenu with horizontal poster carousels
    - Category tabs: Animation, Family, Kids TV, Disney Style, G & PG
    - Click any category to open full grid view with 30 items
    - Grid view with Next/Prev page navigation
    - Detail popup with Play, Trailer, Favorite buttons
  * All three main sections (Movies, TV Shows, Kids) now have consistent UI:
    - Netflix-style submenu → Grid view → Detail popup → Play
  * IMPROVED: Unified browsing experience across all content types

v3.4.4 (January 2026)
================================================================================
  * NEW: Netflix-style grid view when clicking on genres or categories
    - Full page grid with 30 items (6 columns x 5 rows)
    - Next/Prev page navigation buttons with page indicator
    - "Showing X-Y of Z" items display
  * NEW: Detail popup when clicking on a movie/TV show poster in grid
    - Shows full movie/show details (title, year, rating, runtime, genres, plot)
    - Cast and director information
    - PLAY button - starts source search
    - TRAILER button - plays YouTube trailer (requires YouTube addon)
    - FAVORITE button - add/remove from favorites
    - Close button to return to grid
  * IMPROVED: Clicking genre from submenu now opens paginated grid view
  * IMPROVED: Clicking category tab (Popular, Top Rated, etc.) opens paginated grid view
  * IMPROVED: Much better browsing experience with "infinite" pages of content
