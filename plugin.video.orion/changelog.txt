v3.7.6 (April 2026)
================================================================================
  * BETA NOTICE:
       PunchPlay.tv is currently a PLACEHOLDER integration. The full
       Trakt-parity menu and Client ID/Secret support added in v3.7.5 are
       in place, but NOT EVERY ENDPOINT on the PunchPlay backend has been
       implemented yet. Some lists may come back empty or fail until the
       PunchPlay team ships their public API.
       This will be promoted out of BETA once all PunchPlay endpoints are
       live.
  * UI:
       - Main menu shows "PunchPlay [BETA]"
       - PunchPlay submenu opens with a red BETA banner and a yellow
         "not all endpoints live yet" notice
       - Device-code login dialog now reads "PunchPlay Authorization [BETA]"
         and explicitly states it is a placeholder
       - Settings > PunchPlay (BETA) shows a disabled BETA notice line
  * No functional regressions vs 3.7.5.

v3.7.5 (April 2026)
================================================================================
  * NEW:   PunchPlay.tv now has FULL Trakt-parity. The PunchPlay menu mirrors
           the Trakt menu and supports:
              - My Watchlist (Movies / TV Shows) with pagination
              - My Custom Lists  (your lists on PunchPlay)
              - My Liked Lists   (lists you liked, paginated)
              - My Collection    (Movies / TV Shows)
              - Watched          (Movies / TV Shows)
              - Recommendations  (Movies / TV Shows, paginated)
              - Trending / Popular (Movies / TV Shows)
              - Netflix-style PunchPlay submenu (when Netflix skin enabled)
  * NEW:   Right-click context menu on PunchPlay items:
              - Remove from Watchlist
              - Remove from Collection
              - Remove from Watched History
              - Remove from Custom List (your own lists only)
  * NEW:   Auto-mark watched at >=80% progress for both movies and episodes.
  * NEW:   Settings > PunchPlay now accepts a Client ID and Client Secret so
           OAuth-style installs of punchplay.tv are supported. Visit
           https://punchplay.tv/account/apps to create your application and
           copy the Client ID + Client Secret into the settings.
  * NEW:   "Show QR Code" action in Settings > PunchPlay for the link page.

v3.7.4 (April 2026)
================================================================================
  * FIXED: Trakt list items can now be removed via the right-click "Context"
           button. Adds "Remove from..." entries to:
              - My Custom Lists (only when you own the list)
              - My Collection (Movies & TV Shows)
              - Watched History (Movies & TV Shows)
           "Remove from Watchlist" already worked and is preserved.
  * NEW:   PunchPlay.tv integration. Authorize from Settings > PunchPlay or the
           new "PunchPlay" entry in the main menu (device-code login at
           https://punchplay.tv/link). Once connected, episode playback is
           scrobbled to your PunchPlay account in parallel with Trakt.
  * NEW:   Settings > PunchPlay category with Authorize / Logout / Backend URL.
